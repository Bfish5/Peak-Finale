Areas of Expertise

Treatment Specialties

From sports injuries to neuromuscular conditions and proactive wellness, P.E.A.K. offers one-on-one treatment plans tailored to your diagnosis, lifestyle, and goals.

Sports & Orthopedic Rehabilitation

Comprehensive rehab and performance training for active adults, weekend warriors, and athletes returning to sport or staying ahead of injury.

  • Ankle sprains & ligament injuries
  • ACL rehab & post-surgical care
  • Back & neck pain
  • Joint replacements
  • Osteoarthritis-related pain & stiffness
  • Plantar fasciitis & foot pain
  • Shin splints & overuse injuries
  • Shoulder impingement & rotator cuff issues
  • Tennis & golfer’s elbow
  • Speed, agility & post-rehab strength training

Neuromuscular Conditions

Skilled physical therapy to help clients with complex neurological diagnoses maintain safety, function, and independence as long as possible.

  • Alzheimer’s disease & cognitive decline
  • ALS and other motor neuron diseases
  • Cerebral palsy
  • Multiple sclerosis
  • Muscular dystrophies
  • Myasthenia gravis
  • Parkinson’s disease
  • Stroke & post-stroke recovery
  • Traumatic brain injury

Pediatric Physical Therapy

Gentle, family-centered care to support children as they grow, learn new skills, and gain confidence in everyday activities.

  • Cerebral palsy & developmental motor delays
  • Orthopedic and post-surgical conditions
  • Strength, balance, and coordination training

Personal Training & Performance Coaching

Individualized exercise programming, accountability, and expert guidance to help you build strength, improve endurance, and stay active safely.

  • Goal-focused programs built around your life
  • Ongoing coaching and progression
  • Ideal after formal rehab to keep you moving forward

Comprehensive Wellness & Maintenance Programs

An active body tends to stay active. Our ongoing wellness programs are perfect for people who want continued support once rehab ends or who prefer a preventative, movement-first approach.

  • Custom maintenance plans designed around your goals
  • Safe, effective programs to keep you mobile and independent
  • Ideal for adults managing chronic conditions or prior injuries
We participate with Medicare Part B for skilled physical therapy services.
