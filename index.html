About P.E.A.K. Physical Therapy & Performance Training

Care designed around the whole person, not just a diagnosis.

P.E.A.K. is a concierge physical therapy and performance practice focused on helping adults, athletes, and families move with confidence. Whether you are recovering from an injury, managing a medical condition, or planning a proactive wellness routine, your plan is created from the ground up for you.

You work directly with Dr. Kristan Fish for the entire session — never with aides or techs. That uninterrupted time allows for ongoing assessment, hands-on care, and real-time exercise progressions.

We also participate with Medicare Part B for skilled physical therapy services.

Our Mission

To maximize quality of life for every person we work with by restoring movement, reducing pain, and building strength and confidence that lasts well beyond your time in the clinic.

Vision & Inclusivity

We believe everyone deserves expert, one-on-one care — regardless of age, race, religion, marital status, ability, sexual orientation, or gender. We are proud to serve a diverse community in Howard County.
